Welcome to BrainDeck Support

BrainDeck is a beautiful, intuitive flashcard app designed to make studying enjoyable and effective. This support page will help you get the most out of your learning experience.

Getting Started

Creating Your First Flashcard

  1. Open BrainDeck on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap the blue "New Card" button at the bottom right
  3. Enter your question in the "Front" field
  4. Enter the answer in the "Back" field
  5. Optionally, assign the card to a folder
  6. Tap "Save" to create your flashcard

Creating Folders

  1. Tap the "Folder" button at the bottom
  2. Enter a folder name (e.g., "Spanish Vocabulary")
  3. Choose a color to identify your folder
  4. Optionally, nest it inside another folder
  5. Tap "Save"

Studying Your Flashcards

  1. Tap any flashcard to open study mode
  2. Tap the card to flip and reveal the answer
  3. Swipe left/right to navigate between cards
  4. Use Previous/Next buttons for easier navigation

Backup & Restore Guide

Exporting Your Flashcards

  1. Open Settings (gear icon)
  2. Scroll to "Backup & Restore"
  3. Tap "Export Flashcards"
  4. Choose where to save:
    • Save to Files app
    • Save to iCloud Drive
    • AirDrop to another device
    • Email to yourself
    • Share via Messages

Importing Flashcards

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap "Import Flashcards"
  3. Choose import mode:
    • Replace All Data: Deletes current flashcards and replaces with imported ones
    • Merge with Existing: Keeps current flashcards and adds imported ones (no duplicates)
  4. Select your backup file (.json)
  5. Confirm the import

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is BrainDeck free?
A: Yes! BrainDeck is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.
Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: No! BrainDeck works completely offline. All your flashcards are stored locally on your device.
Q: Can I sync my flashcards across multiple devices?
A: Currently, BrainDeck stores data locally on your device. However, you can use the Export/Import feature to transfer flashcards between devices, or rely on iCloud Backup to restore data when setting up a new device.
Q: Will I lose my flashcards if I update the app?
A: No! Your flashcards are safely stored and will remain intact when you update BrainDeck from the App Store.
Q: Will I lose my flashcards if I delete the app?
A: Yes. If you uninstall BrainDeck, all data will be deleted. Use the Export feature to create a backup before uninstalling.
Q: How many flashcards can I create?
A: There's no limit! Create as many flashcards and folders as you need.
Q: Can I share my flashcard decks with friends?
A: Yes! Use the Export feature to create a backup file, then share it via AirDrop, email, or Messages. Your friend can import it into their BrainDeck app.
Q: How do I reorder flashcards?
A: Tap the three-dot menu (‚ãØ) on any flashcard, then use the "Move Up" or "Move Down" buttons to reorder cards within a folder.
Q: Can I add images to flashcards?
A: Currently, BrainDeck supports text-only flashcards. Image support may be added in future updates.
Q: Does BrainDeck collect my data?
A: No! BrainDeck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. We don't use analytics, tracking, or any third-party services.
Q: My device backed up to iCloud. Will my flashcards be backed up too?
A: Yes! If you have iCloud Backup enabled, iOS automatically backs up BrainDeck's data. When you restore from iCloud backup or set up a new device, your flashcards will be restored.
Q: How do I delete a folder?
A: Long-press on a folder to bring up the context menu, then select "Delete Folder." Note: This will also delete all flashcards and subfolders within it.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  • Make sure you have the latest version of BrainDeck from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem persists, contact support (see below)

Import not working

  • Ensure the file is a valid BrainDeck backup (.json file)
  • Check that the file wasn't corrupted during transfer
  • Try exporting a fresh backup and importing that to test

Can't see all my flashcards

  • Check if flashcards are inside collapsed folders
  • Tap folders to expand them and view their contents
  • Look in the "UNORGANIZED" section for cards not in folders

Settings won't scroll

  • Try scrolling with two fingers or using a scroll gesture
  • Look for the scroll indicator on the right side
  • Make sure you're scrolling within the settings area
