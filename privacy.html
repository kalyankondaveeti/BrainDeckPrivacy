Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

TL;DR: BrainDeck.us doesn't collect any of your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Introduction

BrainDeck ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how BrainDeck handles information when you use our iOS application.

TechPrime LLC operates BrainDeck.us with a privacy-first approach. We believe your learning materials should remain completely private and under your control.

Data Collection

BrainDeck.us does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

What we DON'T collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • User account information (no accounts required)
  • Flashcard content or study materials
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Any other personal or non-personal information

Data Storage

All data created in BrainDeck.us is stored exclusively on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults).

What this means:

  • All flashcards, folders, and settings remain on your iPhone or iPad
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • No data is sent to third-party services
  • Your study materials are completely private
  • We have no access to your data whatsoever

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, iOS automatically backs up app data to your personal iCloud account. This is a feature of iOS, not BrainDeck.

Important notes:

  • iCloud backups are controlled by your iOS settings, not by BrainDeck
  • Data backed up to iCloud is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We (TechPrime LLC) do not have access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud backup for BrainDeck in iOS Settings if desired

Export/Import Feature

BrainDeck allows you to export your flashcards to a file and import flashcards from a file. This feature operates entirely locally:

  • Export creates a file on your device that you control
  • You choose where to save or share the exported file (Files app, iCloud Drive, email, etc.)
  • Import reads files from locations you select
  • No data is sent to BrainDeck servers during export or import (we have no servers)

Third-Party Services

BrainDeck does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services (other than your personal iCloud)
  • No tracking or data collection tools

BrainDeck.us operates completely independently with zero external dependencies.

Data Sharing

Since BrainDeck.us doesn't collect any data, we have nothing to share. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties because we simply don't have any data to share.

The only way data leaves your device is if you explicitly choose to export and share flashcards using the Export feature.

Children's Privacy

BrainDeck.us is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

No parental consent is required because no data is collected.

Data Security

Your data's security is ensured by:

  • Local storage: Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
  • iOS protection: Your data is protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID
  • No transmission: No network communication means no interception risk
  • Sandboxing: iOS app sandboxing prevents other apps from accessing data

Your Rights

Since BrainDeck.us doesn't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) aren't applicable in the usual sense. However:

  • Full control: You have complete control over your data at all times
  • Export: You can export all your data using the Export feature
  • Delete: You can delete individual flashcards, folders, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • No account: No account means no data tied to an identity

App Updates

When you update BrainDeck.us from the App Store:

  • Your flashcards and settings are preserved
  • No data is transmitted during the update process
  • The same privacy protections continue to apply

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

If we ever change our data collection practices (which we have no plans to do), we will notify users through an app update and obtain necessary consent.

Legal Compliance

BrainDeck.us complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - USA
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) - USA
  • Apple's App Store Guidelines

Compliance is straightforward because we don't collect any data.
